Music Without Boundaries

We are a global music community and record label specializing in electronic music. At MrFox Music, our mission is to empower the next generation of artists and creators through our unique free-to-use license model.

MrFox Music began as a YouTube channel, driven by the need for high-quality, truly copyright-free music. Our passion for music led us to develop into a music label with a vast catalog of electronic tracks available for content creators. The MrFox Music YouTube channel has grown to be one of the largest in the world, serving as a platform to spotlight new talent.

Today, MrFox Music is the leading copyright-free record label globally, boasting a library of over 1500 songs across diverse genres and moods, with more than 500 billion plays worldwide. Our approach allows independent creators to freely use and monetize our music, provided they give proper credit to the original artists.

We offer a wide array of genres, from House to Dubstep, including Trap, Drum & Bass, Electro Pop, and beyond. Our music has featured in over 250 million videos, used by top creators, streamers, and brands such as MrBeast, The Sidemen, and Chloe Ting.

Our success stems from providing creators on platforms like YouTube and Twitch with free-to-use music, eliminating the fear of copyright strikes. Unlike generic stock music, our tracks come from real artists who tour, have global fan bases, and are well-known names in the industry.
